Passport Application Home Page

Welcome to the Passport Application home page! Here, you can find information about how to apply for a passport, check the status of your application, and more.

To apply for a passport, you will need to complete the following steps:

  1. Gather the required documents, including proof of citizenship and identification.
  2. Complete the passport application form.
  3. Submit your application and required documents in person at a passport acceptance facility.

If you have any questions or concerns about the passport application process, please contact us. We are here to help!

If you have already applied for a passport, you can track the status of your application online.

To check the status of your passport application, you can use the online Passport Application Status tool. You will need to provide your last name, date of birth, and the last four digits of your Social Security number.
